# What It is?
So, SolePlay is a mini touch and play pocket game that you must need to play to save your life before the world ends.
*If you win, you will survive..if you lose you die before the world ends*

# Why we made it?
So, we are the special agents selected to work at Soup's base in China to save the world and the people...So we made this game that let's you survive and saves you even after the world ends if you win, but if you lose then you die before the world ends. so play carefully..you only have 3chances

# How it works?

This tiny tuny game has 8 touch sensors, followed by 8 solenoids on it's frond and further followed by 8 leds. In this game you will have 30seconds and each LED glows randomly and you need to press the touch sensor behind  it to activate the solenoid and the speed increases gradually. If you press the wrogn button 3 times then a bom erupts and you die.






# Specification

* Input: 8 capacitive touch sensor
* MCU: Xiao esp32 c3
* feedback: vibration fead and solonoid movement
* mechanical part: 8 solonoid (each with touch sensor)
* power management: 9 mosfet(8 for solonoid and 1 for capacitor)
* life saving feature: 2 capacitor(that will blust if you lose the game)
